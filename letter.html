My Letter To You

✉
🌸

Valentine's Day · February 2025

Dearest Mom,

I have been thinking about how to say this properly for a while now, and I keep coming back to the same truth: there are no words big enough. But I am going to try anyway, because you deserve to hear it.

You have given me so much more than I ever realised growing up. The safety of knowing someone was always in my corner. The warmth of a home you made feel like the softest place in the world to land. The quiet confidence you built in me without me even noticing you were doing it.

I see it now — all of it. Every sacrifice that looked like just another ordinary Tuesday. Every worry you carried so I would not have to. Every time you put me first without me ever asking you to.

I think about all the little things: the way the house felt when you were in it, the sound of your voice when I called in the middle of something hard, the way you always knew — somehow always knew — when I needed you most.

You are the reason I know what love looks like. Not the grand, dramatic kind — but the real kind. The steady, showing-up, never-giving-up kind. I learned it from watching you.

Thank you, Mom. For everything you are. For everything you have given me. For loving me the way that only you know how. I love you more than I could ever properly say.

All my love, always, Your Child ♥
